The purpose of this lab is to understand video in multimedia presentations and practice with video editing and conversion with different formats.

Explain your work process, including editing on music and video.

  • I decided to use Adobe Spark as my editing tool since it is provided by Seneca and is easy to use.
  • I had a bunch of stop motion drawings that I decided to elevate by making a stop motion video.
  • I used a sound/music that was light and easy to hear off of free music samples which last 1 minute.
  • I used elements of fade in and out on text, removed any background noise, and added transitions.

Explain your thoughts on video creation, such as file size and format.

  • I believe having file size as small as possible is essential to make sure that it is readily available to users. Larger video files can create an extra load on the website and make things slower for users when trying to access the content.

  • MP4 is a standard, so it is more compatible with a wide range of platforms and players. It is the default for web and social media because it produces smaller file sizes with outstanding quality.
