Yellow Warbler (Edited Image)

This is the same picture of a yellow warbler above but edited by Sahil Patel.

    Questions:
  1. What’s your image modification? Any thoughts (why)? Steps?
    2.
    - The image details have been modified and it can be clearly seen by the pixelated result.
    - It image was extremely large in dimensions and was of extreme quality. Therefore, a big change was necessary.
    - The first step was choosing a file editing software, I chose photoshop.
    - The second step was changing the size of the photo (width and height). From 400 x 400 to 10cm x 6cm and scaled to be 400 x 400 again which is why it is so pixelated.
    - The third step was to lower the resolution from 72 to 25 which made all the difference. Then export it and upload it to the website.
  2. What are the differences you notice from the modification (e.g., file size change? Quality change? etc.)
    3.
    - I notice the image is much blurrier because of the modifications made to the size of the image and resolution. So, Quality was affected.
    - The image size was greatly reduced. It was 680.5K and went down to 19.1K which is a big change.
